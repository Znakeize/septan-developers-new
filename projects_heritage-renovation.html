Heritage Renovation

The Heritage Renovation project in Paris, completed in 2021, involved restoring a historic building while incorporating modern amenities. We preserved original features like facades and interiors, adding contemporary touches for functionality.

This renovation balanced heritage conservation with current standards, including energy upgrades and structural reinforcements.
